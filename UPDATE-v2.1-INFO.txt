PERUBAHAN TEKNIS
-----------------
1. Fungsi baru: Fix-Error0x00000040
   - Di PrinterSharingFix-Server.ps1
   - Di PrinterSharingFix-Client.ps1

2. Registry yang dimodifikasi:
   - HKLM\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Print
   - Key: PrintDriverIsolationExecutionPolicy
   - Value: 0 (Disable isolation)

3. Client tambahan:
   - Clear HKCU\Printers\Connections

4. GUI Update:
   - GroupBox height +30px
   - Tombol baru Fix 7/Fix 9
   - Log area disesuaikan
